Ikaze ku Rubuga rw'Amakuru ku Kuhira

Menya uko wakuhira ibimera bitandukanye ukurikije ubwoko bwabyo n'ibidukikije.

Ibyerekeye Urubuga rw'Amakuru ku Kuhira

Urubuga rwacu rwashinzwe hagamijwe gutanga amakuru yizewe kandi afasha abahinzi mu bijyanye no kuhira ibihingwa. Twiyemeje gufasha abahinzi b’Abanyarwanda kumenya uburyo butandukanye bwo kuhira, hakurikijwe ubwoko bw’igihingwa, igihe cy’ihinga n’akarere giherereyemo.

Duhuza ubumenyi bw'ubuhinzi bugezweho n’ikoranabuhanga kugira ngo dukemure ibibazo abahinzi bahura na byo mu gihe cyo kuhira. Urubuga rutanga amakuru ku bimera, uburyo bwo kuhira nka drip, sprinkler, surface na subsurface irrigation, ndetse n’uburyo bwo gukoresha amazi neza.

Intego yacu ni uguteza imbere ubuhinzi burambye, bwongera umusaruro kandi bukoresha amazi mu buryo buboneye. Twizera ko amakuru dusangiza azafasha abahinzi guhitamo uburyo bukwiye bwo kuhira bitewe n’ibyo bahinga n’ibidukikije bibakikije.

Uko Wahira Ibimera Bitandukanye

Imboga

Uburyo bwiza bwo kuhira imboga nk'inyanya, dodo, n'ibirayi.

Imbuto

Gukoresha drip irrigation mu gihingwa cy’imbuto zitandukanye.

Indabo

Hitamo sprinkler cyangwa drip bitewe n’ubwoko bw’indabo.

Uburyo Bukoreshwa mu Kuhira

Drip Irrigation (Kuhira utuyoka)

Ni uburyo bukoreshwa mu kuhira buca munsi y’ubutaka cyangwa hejuru h’inkingi, aho amazi atemba gahoro agera ku mizi y’igihingwa. Bukoresha amazi make kandi butanga umusaruro mwinshi.

Sprinkler (Kuhira nk’imvura)

Ubu buryo busa n’imvura isanzwe, aho amazi asakazwa mu mirima hifashishijwe ibyuma bya sprinkler. Bukoreshwa ahanini ku ndabo n'ibimera bisaba amazi menshi hejuru.

Surface Irrigation (Kuhira usuka ku butaka)

Amazi ashyirwa ku buso bw’ubutaka, akagenda yisuka kugeza ku mizi. Ni bwo buryo bwa kera kandi bworoshye, ariko bukenera amazi menshi.

Subsurface Irrigation (Kuhira munsi y'ubutaka)

Amazi anyuzwa mu miyoboro iri munsi y’ubutaka, agera ku mizi y'ibimera atabanje kujya hejuru. Akarusho ni uko amazi adatakara cyane kandi ubuhinzi bugakorwa neza mu turere dufite amazi make.
